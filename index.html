Recipe #1

SAMOSA

  • Time: 1 HOUR 10 MINUTES
  • Serves: 8 SAMOSA
Ingredients Quantity
plain flour 2 cup
carom seeds ¼ tsp
salt ½ tsp
oil ¼ cup
water ½ cup
cumin 1 tsp
coriander seeds, crushed ½ tsp
fennel ½ tsp
asafoetida pinch
ginger, finely chopped 1 inch
chilli, finely chopped 1
peas ½ cup

Step 1: pinch a ball sized dough and grease with oil.
Step 2: roll the dough into oval shape.
Step 3: now cut it horizontally, diving into 2 equal portions using a knife.
Step 4: grease with water and make cone.
Step 5: stuff 2 tbsp of prepared samosa masala into the cone.
